I want to find out how to refresh a button. I enter in Google:
"how to refresh a button"
..but what I find is a bunch of posts about the "refresh button":
"how to ... refresh button"
just terribly annoying, I cannot find what I need. And of course double quotes don't help at all. Well, a good example.
